About This Clinic

Three standalone sessions designed around one theme: going faster underwater. Each session is complete on its own — but if you attend all three, the progression builds on itself in ways that will change how you swim off every wall for the rest of your career.

What to Bring

Suit, cap, goggles, and fins. Aquavolo DragSox will be provided for Session 2. Bring a water bottle and a snack between sessions if attending multiple. Parents are welcome to watch from the deck.
