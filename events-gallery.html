Splash Aquatics x Seal Beach Clinic
📍 Seal Beach, CA 🏊 All Ages

Tom brought DK Lab to Seal Beach for a full-day clinic hosted by Splash Aquatics in April 2026. Athletes of all ages worked on technique in the water, got one-on-one coaching time with an Olympic gold medalist, and had the chance to hold the hardware. The medals came out. Kids lost their minds.
